Why Growtainer?

Most self-watering planters only solve watering. Growtainer solves nutrition.

  • ✅ Volcanic rock dust for trace minerals (Zn, Se, Mn)
  • ✅ Worm castings for living soil biology
  • ✅ Dolomite layer for calcium & magnesium
  • ✅ Self-dosing nutrients — plant can constantly take what it needs to grow at full capacity
  • ✅ Open-source & free — no lock-in, full adaptability

We don’t just grow food. We grow health, resilience, and autonomy.
